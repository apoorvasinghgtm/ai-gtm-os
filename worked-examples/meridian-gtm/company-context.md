## What we sell

Meridian GTM is an AI-powered sales intelligence platform that compresses the research-to-ready cycle for enterprise B2B sales teams. It ingests account signals from public sources, intent data, CRM history, and call intelligence — and surfaces a prioritized, personalized pre-sale brief for every rep, before every meeting.

**One sentence:** Meridian GTM turns 4 hours of pre-meeting research into a 15-minute rep review — without sacrificing depth or accuracy.

---

## How it works

Meridian connects to a company's existing tech stack (CRM, Gong/Chorus, intent data provider, LinkedIn Sales Navigator) and continuously monitors a defined account universe for buying signals: leadership changes, funding events, technology stack shifts, hiring patterns, competitive activity, and earnings commentary.

When a rep opens an account or schedules a meeting, Meridian generates a pre-sale intelligence brief: a structured document containing account context, stakeholder map, relevant trigger events, likely pain points based on signal patterns, suggested discovery questions, competitive context, and an ROI anchor calibrated to their industry benchmarks.

## Key differentiators

**1. Signal-to-brief automation — not just data aggregation**
Most competitors deliver data. Meridian delivers a brief. The synthesis layer — turning raw signals into a structured, actionable document — is where competitors stop and Meridian starts. Reps don't have to figure out what the signals mean; the brief tells them.

**2. Stack-native, not stack-replacing**
Meridian does not ask sales teams to change their workflow. It enriches the tools they already use — SFDC, HubSpot, Gong, Outreach — rather than adding another interface to manage. Briefs surface inside the CRM record and calendar invite.

**3. Outcome-calibrated ROI anchoring**
Meridian's ROI model is pre-populated with verified industry benchmarks segmented by company size, industry, and deal complexity. Reps do not build ROI from scratch. They customize a pre-built model, which reduces business case build time from days to hours and increases win rates on price-sensitive deals.

## Voice and tone

**Tone:** Direct, credible, practitioner-level. We talk to people who have been in the room — not to people who need to be sold on why sales prep matters.

**Always:**
- Lead with the rep's or manager's problem, not our product
- Use specific numbers — "67% reduction" not "significant reduction"
- Acknowledge the complexity of enterprise sales; don't oversimplify
- Respect the reader's intelligence — they know their business better than we do

**Never:**
- Use "revolutionary," "game-changing," "best-in-class," or "paradigm shift"
- Imply that AI replaces sales judgment — Meridian augments it
- Make claims that aren't backed by data we can cite
- Use jargon without definition in external-facing materials

**Approved terminology:**
- We say "sales intelligence brief," not "AI report"
- We say "signal synthesis," not "data aggregation"
- We say "rep-ready," not "automated"
- We say "augment," not "replace" or "automate away"
