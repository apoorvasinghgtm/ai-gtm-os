# Persona Cards

> **How to use this file:** Document 3–4 buyer personas for your primary ICP. Each card should reflect the real people in the buying committee — economic buyer, champion, technical evaluator, and end user are the most common configuration. Be honest about what each persona cares about; AI outputs calibrated to a sanitized persona will miss. Upload this to your Claude Project.
