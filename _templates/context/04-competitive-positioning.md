# Competitive Positioning

> **How to use this file:** Document your top 3 competitors and your positioning against each. Be honest — acknowledging where competitors are strong makes your positioning more credible and prevents AI from generating overclaims. This file is for internal use only; never upload to public-facing systems. Upload to your Claude Project's knowledge base.
