## Format Standards

### Email / Outbound Copy
- **Length:** [e.g., Under 150 words for cold outreach, under 250 for follow-ups]
- **Structure:** [e.g., One problem, one proof point, one ask — no more]
- **Subject lines:** [e.g., No clickbait, no "quick question," reference something specific to them]
- **CTA:** [e.g., Single, specific ask — never two options]

### Internal Briefs and Documents
- **Length:** [e.g., Executive briefs under 1 page; workflow outputs should be scannable, not paragraphs]
- **Headers:** [e.g., Always use headers for documents longer than 300 words]
- **Bullet points:** [e.g., Use for lists of 3 or more; complete sentences, not fragments]
